8-Week Dynamic Strength & Size Program

Research-backed training for maximal muscle growth and strength gains

Program starts on TUESDAY - Adjust your calendar accordingly!

Program Overview

Weeks 1-2: Foundation

Moderate intensity (70-80% 1RM), higher volume, technique focus

Weeks 3-4: Intensification

Increasing intensity (75-85% 1RM), moderate volume

Weeks 5-6: Peak Strength

High intensity (80-90% 1RM), lower volume, max effort

Week 7: Deload

Recovery week (60-70% 1RM), reduced volume

Week 8: Testing

New 1RM testing or high-intensity singles

Week 8 Testing Notes:

  • Have spotters for all max attempts
  • Don't attempt a weight unless previous felt solid
  • 3 attempts maximum per lift - quality over quantity
  • Record all attempts and use for next cycle planning
  • Celebrate PRs but analyze missed lifts constructively
  • Take 3-5 days off before starting next program
